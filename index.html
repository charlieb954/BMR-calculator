Calculated using the Harris-Benedict BMR formula, expand to see:

Men: BMR = 66.47 + (6.24 × weight in pounds) + (12.7 × height in inches) – (6.755 × age in years)

Women: BMR = 655.1 + (4.35 × weight in pounds) + (4.7 × height in inches) – (4.7 × age in years)


In order to account for activity levels, the resulting BMR will be multiplied by 1.2, 1.375, 1.55, 1.725, 1.9 retrospectively
